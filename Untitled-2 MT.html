PS4

The PS5 is the newest sony console and was released as of November 12 of 2020. It may be tempting to go out and buy the newest console on release date but first consider some things first. The pricing, the PS5 comes in two modules, a digital only for $400 and the standard modules for $500. Don’t forget the number of add-ons that you might want. You should also look for the inevitability to pick one up on sale. The memory difference when compared to the PS5 is rather substantial but if you're looking at storage the PS4 won’t disappoint.
